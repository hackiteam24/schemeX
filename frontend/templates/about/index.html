Our Mission

Bridging the digital divide for rural India

SchemeX AI is dedicated to making government services accessible to every citizen, regardless of their language, literacy level, or digital proficiency. We believe that technology should empower, not exclude.

Our AI-powered platform breaks down barriers by providing voice-first, multilingual assistance that helps rural citizens discover, understand, and apply for government welfare schemes with ease.

Why SchemeX Exists

Government welfare information can be hard to navigate when scheme rules, documents, and application steps are spread across different places.

SchemeX AI brings discovery, eligibility checks, applications, documents, dashboard tracking, and assistant support into one responsive experience.

The interface is designed to be clear on phones, comfortable for first-time users, and honest when data is not available yet.
